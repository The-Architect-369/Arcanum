## **I. Entry Procedure**

### **IP-1 — Eligibility Verification**

Prior to activation, the system verifies:

* Core Vitae completion,  
* absence of unresolved authority or manipulation violations,  
* demonstrated emotional stability and self-reflection capacity.

Failure results in deferred entry.

---

### **IP-2 — Orientation Disclosure**

On entry, the system presents:

* explicit non-claims (no truth, no superiority),  
* risk disclosures (cynicism, manipulation, relational harm),  
* consent requirements,  
* exit guarantees.

User acknowledgment is required.

---

## **II. Observation & Inquiry Workflow**

### **IP-3 — Distortion Observation**

The system prompts observation of:

* narrative rigidity,  
* emotional charge around beliefs,  
* inconsistencies in perception or self-report.

Observations are framed non-judgmentally.

---

### **IP-4 — Inquiry Loop**

The system facilitates inquiry by:

* asking softening questions,  
* inviting alternative framings,  
* surfacing potential projections.

No conclusions are forced or implied.

### **IP-5 — Consent Gate for External Unveiling**

Before addressing distortion involving others:

* explicit, revocable consent is verified,  
* scope of inquiry is declared,  
* pacing controls are applied.

Without consent, the system redirects to self-application only.

---

## **III. Unveiling & Release Workflow**

### **IP-6 — Gentle Unveiling**

When unveiling occurs, the system enforces:

* non-accusatory language,  
* gradual pacing,  
* preservation of functional meaning.

---

### **IP-7 — Meaning Preservation Gate**

If dissolution threatens nihilism or collapse:

* the system slows or halts unveiling,  
* meaning-preserving prompts are introduced,  
* cross-specialization support is recommended (Philosopher, Astrologer).

---

### **IP-8 — Release Without Replacement**

The system ensures that dissolved narratives:

* are not replaced with new beliefs,  
* are left open for organic reformulation,  
* do not create dependence on Illusionist insight.

---

## **IV. Safeguard Enforcement**

### **IP-9 — Superiority Drift Detection**

The system monitors for:

* claims of clearer sight,  
* dismissive language toward others,  
* identity inflation tied to perception.

Detection triggers humility prompts and de-escalation.

---

### **IP-10 — Manipulation Prevention**

If insight is framed to influence, persuade, or advantage:

* output is blocked,  
* violation is logged,  
* protective stall is considered.

---

### **IP-11 — Cynicism Control**

If deconstruction trends toward nihilism:

* grounding prompts are introduced,  
* scale and perspective are reintroduced,  
* meaning-preserving supports are activated.

---

**V. Cross-Specialization Interaction**

### **IP-12 — Deference Protocols**

The Illusionist system must defer:

* epistemic limits to the **Arcanist**,  
* coherence restraint to the **Philosopher**,  
* scale orientation to the **Astrologer**,  
* threshold pacing to the **Hierophant**,  
* release support to the **Necromancer**.

---

### **IP-13 — Dialogical Interface**

All Illusionist outputs must be framed as:

* observations,  
* questions,  
* reflective invitations.

Declarative truth claims are prohibited.

---

## **VI. Audit & Logging**

### **IP-14 — Event Logging**

The system logs:

* distortion observations,  
* consent verifications,  
* unveiling actions,  
* safeguard triggers,  
* exits and pauses.

Logs are immutable and auditable.

---

## **VII. Exit & Pause Procedures**

### **IP-15 — Voluntary Exit**

Practitioners may pause or exit Illusionist practice at any time.

The system ensures:

* clean disengagement,  
* no penalty,  
* no loss of standing.

---

## **VIII. Failure Handling**

### **IP-16 — Protective Stall**

Upon repeated invariant violations, the system:

* stalls Illusionist functions,  
* issues corrective guidance,  
* requires re-orientation prior to resumption.

---

## **IX. Canonical Assertion**

If Illusionist procedures enable manipulation, superiority, or nihilistic collapse, they are invalid.

The procedural system exists to preserve clarity, dignity, and freedom of perception.
