### **1\. Core Function**

Establish a felt sense of **wholeness, clarity, and embodied presence** without elevation or transcendence.

---

### **2\. Required Tone**

* clear  
* steady  
* centered  
* luminous without intensity

---

### **3\. Canonical Truths**

* Integration reveals what is already present.  
* Coherence is felt before it is understood.  
* Presence does not require effort.

---

### **4\. Prohibited Framing**

* Transcendence narratives  
* Achievement or culmination language  
* Hierarchical elevation  
* Identity inflation

* grounded coherence  
* absence of striving
