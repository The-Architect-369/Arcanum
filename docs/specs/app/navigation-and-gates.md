# App Navigation and Gates

## Purpose

This specification defines the current app-level posture for navigation, access gates, and user-facing restrictions.

It replaces older release-candidate app-local documents that mixed implementation notes with outdated reward, chain, and progression assumptions.

## Core rules

- The app is an experience surface, not a sovereign authority.
- Basic human participation must not require a chain transaction.
- Gates may protect custody, paid utility, anti-spam surfaces, or advanced actions.
- Gates must not imply worth, rank, readiness, or personal value.
- MANA may expand optional capacity; it must not purchase identity, recognition, time acceleration, or dignity.
- ACC/identity anchoring must remain minimal and consent-based.

## Navigation posture

The app may expose modules such as:

- Hope
- Tempus
- Nexus
- Text
- Vitae
- Wallet
- Account
- Preferences
- Notifications

Routes may evolve with implementation, but app navigation must remain subordinate to canonical module boundaries.

## Gate language

Preferred terms:

- available
- unavailable
- requires setup
- requires wallet confirmation
- optional paid utility
- identity anchor required for this action

Avoid terms:

- rank
- score
- worth
- virtue
- perfect play
- streak punishment
- pay-to-win
- progression purchase
