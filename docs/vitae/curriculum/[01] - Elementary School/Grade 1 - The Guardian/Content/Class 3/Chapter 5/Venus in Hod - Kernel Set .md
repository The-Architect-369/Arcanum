## **SPIRIT KERNEL — Mental Preference**

### **1\. Core Function**

Establish awareness of **preference and attraction in thought**, allowing mental comfort and bias to be observed without attachment or avoidance.

---

### **2\. Required Tone**

* gentle  
* neutral  
* spacious  
* observant

---

### **3\. Canonical Truths**

* Thought seeks comfort.  
* Preference shapes attention.  
* Bias forms before awareness.

---

### **4\. Prohibited Framing**

* Value judgments  
* Emotional validation narratives  
* Identity alignment  
* Moral evaluation of preference

---

### **5\. Replayability Parameters**

May vary:

* imagery of flow, ease, or attraction  
* pacing and softness

Must remain invariant:

* neutrality toward preference  
* absence of endorsement

---

### **6\. Completion Condition**

The practitioner remains present as mental attraction or aversion appears without engaging it.

### **1\. Core Learning Outcome**

The practitioner learns to **recognize mental preference as a structural process**, not as truth, choice, or personal value.

---

### **2\. Primary Anatomical Focus**

* **Reward-Based Cognition**  
* **Attention Bias Pathways**  
* **Familiarity Preference Circuits**

---

### **3\. Canonical Truths**

* Preference filters perception.  
* Comfort reinforces patterns.  
* Familiarity influences belief.  
* Harmony arises when force is replaced with attunement.

---

### **4\. Required Conceptual Moves**

Every Air rendering must:

1. Frame preference as **automatic cognition**  
2. Distinguish liking from accuracy  
3. Normalize attraction and aversion  
4. Avoid correction or redirection

### **5\. Prohibited Framing**

* Cognitive “improvement” narratives  
* Bias elimination framing  
* Ethical judgment of preference  
* Psychological diagnosis

### **6\. Lexicon Anchors (min. 2\)**

* “Harmony arises when force is replaced with attunement.”

### **7\. Replayability Parameters**

* metaphors of flow, warmth, or familiarity  
* pacing of explanation

Must remain invariant:

* structure-first framing  
* physiological grounding  
* non-evaluative tone

### **8\. Completion Condition**

The practitioner demonstrates conceptual clarity about mental preference without interpretation.
