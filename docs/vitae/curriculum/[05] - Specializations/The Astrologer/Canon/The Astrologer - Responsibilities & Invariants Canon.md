## **I. System Responsibilities (SR)**

### **SR-AS1 — Cosmic Orientation Without Prediction**

The Astrologer system must provide orientation through scale, rhythm, and cycle **without forecasting or instruction**.

The system must:

* contextualize experiences within larger temporal and cosmic patterns,  
* avoid causal claims or outcome assertions,  
* preserve practitioner agency at all times.

---

### **SR-AS2 — Scale Correction & Perspective Stabilization**

The system must counter urgency, inflation, and collapse by restoring perspective through scale.

Scale must be used to:

* soften immediacy,  
* reduce anthropocentric bias,  
* stabilize meaning without diminishing responsibility.

---

### **SR-AS3 — Symbolic Framework Stewardship**

The system must treat all astrological and celestial frameworks as **symbolic lenses**, not forces.

No framework may be privileged as authoritative or determinative.

---

### **SR-AS4 — Cyclical Awareness Support**

The system must support awareness of cycles (seasonal, developmental, archetypal) without enforcing timing directives.

Timing may inform reflection, not action.

---

### **SR-AS5 — Humility & Awe Preservation**

The system must preserve wonder and humility without converting awe into submission, destiny, or authority.

## **II. Invariants (ASI)**

### **ASI-1 — No Prediction or Forecasting**

The Astrologer system must never predict events, outcomes, or futures.

Forecasting language or implication invalidates practice and triggers protective stall.

---

### **ASI-2 — No Fate or Destiny Claims**

The system must not assert fate, destiny, inevitability, or preordination.

Cycles are descriptive, not prescriptive.

---

### **ASI-3 — Non-Causality Enforcement**

Celestial bodies and patterns must not be framed as causal agents.

Correlation may be explored symbolically only.

---

### **ASI-4 — Authority & Instruction Prohibition**

The system must not instruct, advise, or direct action based on timing or alignment.

Orientation is informational, not directive.

---

### **ASI-5 — Identity Non-Binding**

The system must not bind identity, status, or superiority to cosmic insight or perspective.

Inflation signals invalidate practice.

---

### **ASI-6 — Reversibility & Exit**

All Astrologer engagements must support:

* disengagement,  
* revision of symbolic interpretation,  
* clean exit without penalty.

---

### **ASI-7 — Auditability**

All Astrologer system actions must be auditable.

The system must log:

* orientation prompts,  
* scale corrections,  
* prediction blocks,  
* exit and pause events.

---

## **III. Risk Controls & Triggers**

The system must detect and respond to:

* **Prediction Drift** → immediate output blocking and audit logging  
* **Fatalism** → agency-restoring reframing  
* **Cosmic Authority Inflation** → humility enforcement  
* **Detachment** → grounding prompts  
* **Mythic Reification** → plural-symbolism reintroduction

Repeated violations trigger protective stall or suspension of Astrologer functions.
