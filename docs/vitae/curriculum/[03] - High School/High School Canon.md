# High School Canon
## **High School Canon (Grades IX–X)**

**Status:** Canonical · Public-Facing Summary with Internal Authority

**Scope:** Grades IX–X (The Oracle, The Adept)

**Audience:** Practitioners, builders, auditors, and future stewards of the Arcanum

---

## **I. Purpose of the High School**

The High School of the Arcanum Vitae exists to **stabilize creation itself**.

Where the Elementary School stabilizes the self, and the Intermediate School stabilizes systems, the High School confronts the practitioner with:

* generative influence  
* pattern-level consequence  
* responsibility for effects beyond the self or immediate systems

High School is not about ascension. It is about **containment of creative power**.

---

## **II. Non-Negotiable Principles**

The High School enforces the following principles across both grades:

1. **Creation Without Domination**

   * Influence may occur; control may not.  
2. **Pattern Awareness Over Intervention**

   * Seeing precedes acting.  
3. **Responsibility Over Outcome**

   * Effects matter more than success.  
4. **Restraint at Scale**

   * The larger the effect, the greater the constraint.  
5. **Humility as Law**

   * Mastery never becomes superiority.

These principles are enforced procedurally, not symbolically.

---

## **III. Grade Progression Overview**

### **Grade IX — The Oracle**

**Function:** Pattern perception and foresight without prophecy

The Oracle establishes:

* recognition of emergent patterns  
* awareness of second- and third-order effects  
* perception without prediction

The Oracle ensures the practitioner can see without declaring truth.

---

### **Grade X — The Adept**

**Function:** Creative stabilization and governance readiness

The Adept establishes:

* synthesis of all prior grades  
* containment of generative capability  
* readiness to steward advanced domains

The Adept ensures the practitioner can create without distortion.

## **IV. What the High School Does NOT Do**

The High School does not:

* grant prophecy or certainty  
* crown leaders or authorities  
* validate destiny narratives  
* permit unchecked creation  
* replace ethical constraints

Any system that enables these outcomes is invalid.

---

## **V. Completion Criteria**

A practitioner is considered to have completed the High School when:

* both grades are completed canonically  
* no unresolved pattern-level violations remain  
* all prior grade dependencies are stable

Completion signifies **readiness for specialization**, not completion of learning.

---

## **VI. Transition to the Adept Threshold**

Completion of High School unlocks:

* the **Adept Threshold Canon**  
* eligibility for post-grade specialization paths

No practitioner may access specialization content prior to this threshold.

## **VII. Canonical Assertion**

If an implementation:

* enforces the principles and constraints defined herein  
* correctly gates progression through Grades IX–X  
* prevents domination, prophecy, and authority formation

Then it is compliant with the **High School Canon**.

If not, it is invalid regardless of outcomes or user experience.
