### **1\. Core Function**

Establish a felt sense of **space, possibility, and safe expansion** without excess or loss of grounding.

---

### **2\. Required Tone**

* open  
* steady  
* generous  
* grounded

---

### **3\. Canonical Truths**

* Expansion requires support.  
* Capacity grows when conditions allow it.  
* Growth is safest when it is gradual.

---

### **4\. Prohibited Framing**

* Limitless growth narratives  
* Overwhelm or excess  
* Spiritual bypassing  
* Inflation or grandiosity

---

### **5\. Replayability Parameters**

May vary:

* visual scale  
* spacious imagery  
* auditory breadth

Must remain invariant:

* grounded expansion  
* sense of safety

---

### **6\. Completion Condition**

The practitioner remains present with the sense of space without dissociation or urgency.

## **AIR KERNEL — Capacity & Endurance Awareness**

### **1\. Core Learning Outcome**

The practitioner learns to **notice capacity in the body** and recognize the difference between sustainable expansion and overextension.

---

### **2\. Primary Anatomical Focus**

* **Cardiovascular System**  
  * heart  
  * circulation  
* **Respiratory System**  
  * lung capacity  
  * breath depth  
* **Musculoskeletal Endurance**  
  * postural support

---

### **3\. Canonical Truths**

* Capacity is felt, not forced.  
* Endurance grows through regulation.  
* Expansion without structure leads to strain.  
* The body signals when it has room to grow.

---

### **4\. Required Conceptual Moves**

Every Air rendering must:

1. Frame expansion as **responsive**, not ambitious  
2. Emphasize listening for limits  
3. Distinguish growth from overreach  
4. Normalize gradual increase in capacity

---

### **5\. Prohibited Framing**

* “More is always better”  
* Performance endurance metrics  
* Achievement-oriented stamina  
* Competitive expansion narratives

---

### **6\. Lexicon Anchors (min. 2\)**

* “Structure is not restriction; it is support.”  
* “Boundaries allow strength to endure.”  
* “Stability emerges through repetition.”

---

### **7\. Replayability Parameters**

May vary:

* metaphors of space or growth  
* pacing of explanation  
* anatomical emphasis (breath vs circulation)

Must remain invariant:

* sustainable expansion framing  
* anatomical focus  
* non-judgmental tone

---

### **8\. Completion Condition**

The practitioner demonstrates conceptual clarity about capacity and sustainability before proceeding.

---
