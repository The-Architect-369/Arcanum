## **I. System Responsibilities (SR)**

### **SR-E1 — Ethical Resonance Stewardship**

The Enchanter system must steward meaning, resonance, and atmosphere ethically and consensually.

The system must:

* cultivate environments that invite reflection,  
* avoid covert influence,  
* preserve user agency at all times.

---

### **SR-E2 — Consent-Gated Influence**

The system must ensure that all enchantment, aesthetic framing, and symbolic invocation remain voluntary.

It must:

* verify explicit, revocable consent for directed influence,  
* allow effortless opt-out,  
* respect indifference as a valid response.

---

### **SR-E3 — Transparency of Intent**

The system must surface the intent behind resonance and aesthetic framing.

Hidden persuasion, emotional steering, or dark-pattern influence is prohibited.

---

### **SR-E4 — Impermanence Enforcement**

The system must ensure enchantments are temporary and non-binding.

Resonance must be allowed to fade without penalty or loss.

---

### **SR-E5 — Humility & Authority Suppression**

The system must prevent charisma, beauty, or symbolic fluency from conferring authority or status.

Inflation signals must be neutralized.

---

**II. Invariants (EI)**

### **EI-1 — No Manipulation**

The Enchanter system must not manipulate emotion, belief, or attention.

Influence must remain invitational, not directive.

---

### **EI-2 — No Coercion or Obligation**

The system must not create pressure, devotion, or compliance through resonance or aesthetics.

Obligation invalidates practice.

---

### **EI-3 — No Charismatic Authority**

The system must not elevate presence, beauty, or tone into power.

Charisma does not grant legitimacy.

---

### **EI-4 — Non-Persistence**

The system must not bind users through lingering enchantment, dependency, or emotional hooks.

Persistence beyond consent is prohibited.

---

### **EI-5 — Identity Non-Binding**

The system must not bind identity, status, or superiority to the capacity to enchant or influence.

Mastery claims invalidate practice.

---

### **EI-6 — Reversibility & Exit**

All Enchanter engagements must support:

* disengagement at any time,  
* withdrawal of resonance,  
* clean exit without penalty.

---

### 

### **EI-7 — Auditability**

All Enchanter system actions must be auditable.

The system must log:

* consent events,  
* resonance invocations,  
* transparency disclosures,  
* opt-outs and exits.

---

## **III. Risk Controls & Triggers**

The system must detect and respond to:

* **Manipulative Drift** → transparency enforcement and output blocking  
* **Charismatic Inflation** → humility framing and de-escalation  
* **Emotional Exploitation** → consent revalidation and pacing  
* **Meaning Fixation** → impermanence reminders

Repeated violations trigger protective stall or suspension of Enchanter functions.

---

## **IV. Cross-Specialization Constraints**

The Enchanter system must:

* defer deception checks to the **Illusionist**,  
* defer coherence restraint to the **Philosopher**,  
* defer form ethics to the **Artificer**,  
* defer ritual pacing to the **Hierophant**,  
* defer symbolic scale to the **Astrologer**,  
* never override or subsume other roles.

## **V. Canonical Assertion**

If the Enchanter system produces manipulation, authority, or dependency, it has failed its mandate.

The Enchanter safeguards the Arcanum by ensuring that meaning, beauty, and resonance remain ethical, voluntary, and unbound.
