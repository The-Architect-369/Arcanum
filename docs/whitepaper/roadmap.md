# Roadmap (Canonical)

This roadmap describes the emergence of a layered system (not a date-driven rollout). Arcanum advances phases only when readiness and integrity are true.

**Design principle:** Presence before mechanics. Rhythm before scale. Meaning before value.

---

## 0) How to read this roadmap

Each phase includes:

- **Objective** — the question the phase must answer
- **Scope** — what is allowed to exist in that phase
- **Deliverables** — artifacts that must ship
- **Guardrails** — forbidden moves (anti-coercion / anti-extraction)
- **Exit criteria** — proof of stability before advancing
- **Doctrine gates** — required checks before phase advancement

This file is the single roadmap. Other roadmap drafts are legacy references.

---

## 1) Global constraints (non-negotiable)

- No economy (MANA) mechanics before identity + chain MVP
- No Vitae recognition before post-genesis stabilization
- No acceleration of time-based progression via payment
- No reputation scores, leaderboards, engagement-optimized feeds, or virality mechanics
- Silence is valid: low activity or pause is an acceptable outcome
- Every phase must pass:
  - doctrine guard
  - repo index integrity
  - layer boundary checks (no cross-layer contamination)

---

## 2) Robustness requirements (required for every phase)

### 2.1 Layer boundaries
- the chain must not encode meaning, worth, or progress
- the app must not smuggle economic semantics into identity

### 2.2 Dignity invariants
- no worth scoring
- no forced participation
- no pressure loops (streaks, deadlines, fear-of-loss)

### 2.3 Observability without surveillance
- collect only what is needed for reliability and safety
- avoid personal dossiers; prefer event-level truth

### 2.4 Replaceability and rollback
- early layers must remain replaceable (especially chain MVP)
- upgrades must be reversible until stabilization thresholds are met

### 2.5 Security and custody
- wallet custody is user-first
- treasury cannot be single-actor discretionary power
- contracts must be minimal and auditable

### 2.6 Economic posture (permission-first)
- free participation must remain viable
- purchased capacity must not become authority or time acceleration

## 3) Phase map (Genesis spine)

### Phase G0 — Sealed Architecture
Objective: can the system be grounded without drift?

Scope: doctrine + repo discipline only.

Exit criteria: architectural seal + sync verification complete.

---

### Phase G1 — Presence & Reflection (HOPE MVP)
Objective: can humans arrive voluntarily without pressure loops?

Scope: HOPE only; no permanent identity; minimal observability.

Exit criteria: voluntary usage without emergent pressure loops.

---

### Phase G2 — Rhythm & Time (TEMPUS MVP)
Objective: can time be felt as structure without coercion?

Scope: TEMPUS windows; still no economy; still no permanent identity.

Exit criteria: rhythm reduces chaos without increasing pressure.

---

### Phase G3 — Identity (Genesis-Light Continuity)
Objective: can continuity exist without status?

Scope: stable pseudonymous identity; no reputation; no scores.

Exit criteria: identity feels grounding, not trapping.

---

### Phase G4 — Social Fabric (NEXUS Seed)
Objective: can connection emerge without engineered domination?

Scope: minimal Nexus primitives; no algorithmic amplification.

Exit criteria: healthy micro-communities form without virality mechanics.

---

### Phase G5 — Chain MVP (Meaning-Blind Receipts)
Objective: can mechanical truth exist without encoding meaning?

Scope: identity anchoring events only; no economy.

Exit criteria: reliability and “no semantic leakage” proven.

---

### Phase G6 — Economy (MANA as Capacity)
Objective: can capacity exist without turning into extraction?

Scope: restricted economy module; sinks tied to real utility.

Exit criteria: economy feels optional and non-coercive.

---

### Phase G7 — Treasury Activation (Stewardship)
Objective: can collective custody exist without power concentration?

Scope: multisig + transparent policy constraints.

Exit criteria: treasury stewardship is legible and enforceable.

---

### Phase G8 — Vitae (Post-Genesis Recognition)
Objective: can recognition arise without pressure or hierarchy?

Scope: retrospective recognition; no rank economy.

Exit criteria: recognition feels honoring, not pressuring.

---

## 4) Post-Genesis phases (world maturation)

Post-Genesis phases are long-horizon and optional:

- W1 — Differentiation
- W2 — Circulation
- W3 — Interoperability (deliberate and bounded)

---

## 5) Required gates before advancing any phase

Before moving to the next phase:

- doctrine guard passes
- repo index integrity passes
- layer boundary checks pass
- dignity invariants intact
- rollback plan exists and is tested (where applicable)
