# Governance Councils

Status: canonical-draft

## Purpose

Defines the review and continuity bodies that evaluate responsibility before governance execution.

## Steward Review Council

Responsibilities:
- V2 recognition review
- V3 recognition review
- stewardship evaluation
- module responsibility review

Cannot:
- move treasury funds
- authorize protocol upgrades

## Treasury Review Council

Responsibilities:
- treasury proposal review
- allocation review
- treasury auditing
- conflict disclosure review

Cannot:
- execute unilateral treasury actions

## Protocol Review Council

Responsibilities:
- ARCnet review
- ChainCode review
- MANA review
- release review
- upgrade readiness review

Cannot:
- bypass governance approval

## Continuity Council

Responsibilities:
- Architect Delegate review
- Succession review
- emergency continuity planning
- repository continuity

Cannot:
- claim ownership of Arcanum
- activate succession without constitutional procedure

## General Rules

All councils:
- operate transparently where possible
- generate review records
- remain subordinate to doctrine
- may recommend but not unilaterally redefine constitutional invariants
